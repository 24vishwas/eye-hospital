Opticals

Annapoorna Eye Hospital also has a well-equipped optical centre called Annapoorna Opticals, which offers a wide range of high-quality eyewear, including prescription glasses, contact lenses, and sunglasses. Our experienced optometrists provide comprehensive eye exams to help patients choose the right eyewear that suits their needs and preferences. We also offer advanced lens technology and customised lens designs to provide the best vision correction possible. At Annapoorna Opticals, we are committed to providing the highest level of customer service and quality products to help our patients maintain optimal eye health and clear vision.
