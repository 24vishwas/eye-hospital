Eye Care

We provide excellent eye care our specialities include

  • Cataract & IOL Services

    At Annapoorna Eye Hospital, we offer comprehensive cataract diagnosis and treatment services using state-of-the-art technology. Our team of experienced ophthalmologists uses micro-incision cataract surgery techniques to remove cataracts and provide the best possible outcomes for our patients. We also offer advanced intraocular lens (IOL) implants to restore vision and improve quality of life for our patients.

  • Glaucoma Services

    At Annapoorna Eye Hospital, we specialise in the diagnosis and treatment of Glaucoma. Our experienced team of ophthalmologists uses advanced technology to diagnose and manage glaucoma, including non-invasive and minimally invasive treatment options such as laser therapy and surgery. Our goal is to prevent vision loss and improve the quality of life for our patients with glaucoma.

  • Laser correction

    At Annapoorna Eye Hospital, we offer advanced laser correction services to help patients achieve clear vision without glasses or contact lenses. Our experienced team of ophthalmologists uses the latest laser technology to correct vision problems such as nearsightedness, farsightedness, and astigmatism. We provide a personalised approach to laser correction, ensuring that each patient receives the most suitable treatment plan for their specific needs.

  • Diabetic Retinopathy

    At Annapoorna Eye Hospital, we specialise in the diagnosis and management of Diabetic Retinopathy. Our experienced ophthalmologists use advanced imaging technology and treatment options to prevent and manage diabetic retinopathy, including laser therapy and intravitreal injections. Our goal is to prevent vision loss and improve the quality of life for our patients with diabetes. We also provide comprehensive diabetes education to help our patients manage their condition and prevent the progression of diabetic retinopathy.

  • Cornea & Refractive

    At Annapoorna Eye Hospital, we offer comprehensive cornea and refractive services to help patients achieve clear vision. Our experienced ophthalmologists use the latest technology to diagnose and treat corneal diseases and conditions, including corneal transplants and cross-linking. We also offer advanced refractive surgery options, such as LASIK and PRK, to correct vision problems and reduce the need for glasses or contact lenses. Our goal is to provide personalised care and help each patient achieve optimal visual outcomes.

  • Retina Services (Up- coming)

    At Annapoorna Eye Hospital, we specialise in the diagnosis and treatment of retinal diseases and conditions. Our experienced ophthalmologists use state-of-the-art imaging technology and treatment options, including intravitreal injections and laser therapy, to manage retinal disorders such as age-related macular degeneration, retinal detachments, and other retinal diseases. Our goal is to prevent vision loss and improve the quality of life for our patients with retinal conditions. We provide personalised care and treatment plans to ensure the best possible outcomes for each patient.

  • Squint

    At Annapoorna Eye Hospital, we offer specialised services for the diagnosis and management of squint or strabismus. Our experienced team of ophthalmologists uses advanced diagnostic tools to evaluate and diagnose squint and provides a range of treatment options, including non-surgical approaches such as vision therapy and surgical options such as muscle surgery. Our goal is to provide personalised care and help our patients achieve optimal visual outcomes and improve their quality of life.

  • Screening and advanced surgical Management

    At Annapoorna Eye Hospital, we offer comprehensive eye screening services to help detect eye diseases and conditions at an early stage. Our screening services are designed to cater to all age groups and demographics, including children and the elderly. We also offer advanced surgical management options for various eye conditions, including cataracts, glaucoma, and corneal diseases. Our experienced ophthalmologists use the latest surgical techniques and technology to provide the best possible outcomes for our patients. Our goal is to provide personalised care and treatment plans to help our patients achieve optimal visual health.

  • Pediatrics Ophthalmology

    At Annapoorna Eye Hospital, we understand the importance of early detection and treatment of eye conditions in children. Our paediatric ophthalmology services are designed to cater to the unique needs of children, from comprehensive eye exams to the diagnosis and management of eye diseases and conditions. Our experienced ophthalmologists use specialised equipment and techniques to make eye exams comfortable and non-invasive for children. We provide personalised care and treatment plans to help children achieve optimal visual health and development. Our goal is to ensure that children receive the best possible eye care and grow up with healthy eyes and clear vision.

  • Vision Testing

    At Annapoorna Eye Hospital, we offer comprehensive vision testing services to help evaluate the visual acuity and overall health of our patients' eyes. Our experienced optometrists use advanced diagnostic tools and techniques to measure visual acuity, test for colour blindness, and detect refractive errors such as myopia, hyperopia, and astigmatism. We also offer customised prescription glasses and contact lenses to help our patients achieve clear and comfortable vision. Our goal is to provide personalised care and help our patients maintain optimal visual health and clarity.

  • Dilation, Slit Lamp Examination.

    At Annapoorna Eye Hospital, we offer comprehensive vision testing services to help evaluate the visual acuity and overall health of our patients' eyes. Our experienced optometrists use advanced diagnostic tools and techniques to measure visual acuity, test for colour blindness, and detect refractive errors such as myopia, hyperopia, and astigmatism. We also offer customised prescription glasses and contact lenses to help our patients achieve clear and comfortable vision. Our goal is to provide personalised care and help our patients maintain optimal visual health and clarity.

  • Tonometry (Eye Pressure Check)

    Tonometry is a non-invasive diagnostic test used to measure the intraocular pressure (IOP) of the eye. At Annapoorna Eye Hospital, we use advanced tonometry techniques to accurately measure IOP and detect conditions such as glaucoma, which can cause damage to the optic nerve and lead to vision loss. Our experienced ophthalmologists use specialised equipment and techniques to make the tonometry test comfortable and non-invasive for our patients. We provide personalised care and treatment plans to help our patients maintain optimal eye health and prevent vision loss.

  • Colour vision testing

    Colour vision testing is an important diagnostic tool used to evaluate a patient's ability to distinguish different colours. At Annapoorna Eye Hospital, we offer comprehensive colour vision testing services to help identify colour blindness and other visual impairments. Our experienced optometrists use specialised tests and techniques to accurately evaluate a patient's colour vision and provide personalised treatment plans to address any issues. We strive to provide compassionate care and help our patients maintain healthy vision and optimal quality of life.
